Mestre Bimba (порт. - "Мастер писька")

Manoel dos Reis Machado (Salvador, Bahía; born on the 23rd of November 1899 in Goiânia;died on the 15th of February 1974)

Widely known as Mestre Bimba, was the master of Capoeira and created a capoeira style called 'regional'

His name apelido Mestre Bimba was given by his parents at the moment of birth. They were wishing for a boy to be born and waited for it so much that when they saw a child's genitals the father yelled "Bimba" out loud. The word "Bimba" in brazilian portuguese language means "a small penis"
